# Tech Talk & Certification Insights
### Kick-off with the Final Showdown between 2 AWS Cloud Architect Teams

### Purpose of the Event

- To better understand automated security solutions on AWS and how to optimize security workflows.
- To grasp the correct mindset regarding SLAs and effective system monitoring strategies in the Cloud environment.
- To equip knowledge, experience, and the most optimal strategy for the AWS Cloud Practitioner certification exam.

### List of Speakers

- **Mr. Thinh Nguyen** - DevOps/DevSecOps/Cloud Engineer at Styl Solutions
- **Mr. Nguyen Huynh Son** 
- **Mr. Ngo Le Tan Huy**

### Highlights

### 1. Speaker Mr. Thinh Nguyen - DevOps/DevSecOps/Cloud Engineer at Styl Solutions

**Topic:** *Securing Your Web Apps With AWS Security Agent*

Mr. Thinh Nguyen shared the challenges in traditional security workflows, especially the lack of real-time responsiveness and high latency when handling security incidents. To address this, he introduced the **AWS Security Agent** solution, featuring highly powerful automation capabilities, including: Security Review, Code Review, and Automated Pentesting. Adopting automation helps teams detect and prevent vulnerabilities early on.

However, the presentation also delved into technical limitations during practical deployment. Specifically, users must pay attention to weaknesses regarding **Authentication Blocks** (custom authentication mechanisms are often easily bypassed if not configured carefully), **Logic Flaws** (business logic vulnerabilities that automated scanning tools struggle to detect), and **Task-hour Accumulation** (the challenge of optimizing resource costs when scanning for security at scale).

**Outcomes or Value Gained:**
Through this sharing session, I realized the importance of security automation in the Cloud era. At the same time, I learned how to view systems from a DevSecOps perspective, understanding that automation tools are not a "silver bullet" but require strict human oversight in complex logic areas.

---

### 2. Speaker Mr. Nguyen Huynh Son - SLA and Monitoring

**Topic:** *From SLA to Monitoring: What Really Matters*

Mr. Nguyen Huynh Son brought a very fundamental perspective on **SLA (Service Level Agreement)** and positioned **Monitoring** not merely as a technical task, but as a part of the enterprise's overall Risk Management process. He introduced a clear management roadmap: **Identify Risk → Monitor Signals → Respond → Improve**.

In particular, he discussed the **Inverted Pyramid Monitoring Strategy**. Accordingly, instead of just focusing on infrastructure monitoring, we need to allocate resources in descending order of priority: **Customer Journey** ⭢ **Business Metrics** ⭢ **Application** ⭢ **Infrastructure** ⭢ **AWS Services**. This ensures that the monitoring system is truly protecting business value.

**Outcomes or Value Gained:**
The presentation helped shift my mindset from "monitoring whether the server is down or not" to "monitoring whether the customer's experience is affected or not." The inverted pyramid mindset is an excellent guiding principle for setting up alert systems effectively, avoiding alert fatigue in real-world projects.

---

### 3. Speaker Mr. Ngo Le Tan Huy

**Topic:** *Inside The Exam: AWS Cloud Practitioner*

Speaker Ngo Le Tan Huy provided an overview of the 4-level AWS certification path, then dove into the structure and core contents of the **AWS Cloud Practitioner** exam. The key knowledge areas to master include: Cloud Concepts, Security & Compliance, Technology, and Billing/Pricing/Support.

Not only discussing theory, he also shared highly effective exam "tips" derived from practical experience. Methods such as **Mapping Keywords** (quickly associating keywords with corresponding AWS services) and **Review Mistakes** (thoroughly analyzing the reasons for incorrect answers) help optimize study time. He also specifically emphasized the **Mindset** when taking the exam: avoid overthinking and be cautious of common **Language Pitfalls** in the exam questions.

**Outcomes or Value Gained:**
Mr. Huy's insights serve as a valuable "survival guide" for anyone preparing for their first AWS certification exam. I learned how to study and prepare more smartly, as well as how to mentally prepare to navigate through the multiple-choice pitfalls of the exam.
