### General Assessment

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment at the company is very friendly and open. The seniors in the team are always ready to enthusiastically support me whenever I encounter difficulties, even outside working hours. The neat and comfortable workspace helps me maximize my focus on work. To increase cohesion, I propose the company could organize more networking sessions or team bonding activities so members can understand each other better.

**2. Support from mentor / team admin**  
My mentor guided me very meticulously, clearly explained professional issues, and always encouraged me to ask questions to dig deeper. The Team Admin also supported me very well with procedures and documents, creating the most favorable conditions for me to complete my work. I particularly appreciate that my Mentor always trusts and allows me to test and solve problems myself instead of just providing ready-made answers.

**3. Alignment between work and major**  
The tasks assigned to me are completely aligned with the foundational knowledge I learned at school, while also helping me expand into new areas that I had never approached before. Thanks to this, I both consolidated my theory and practiced necessary practical skills.

**4. Opportunities to learn & develop skills**  
During the internship, I accumulated many important skills such as using project management tools, effective teamwork skills, and professional communication manners in a corporate environment. The practical experiences shared by my Mentor also helped me orient my future career path more clearly.

**5. Culture & team spirit**  
The company culture is very positive, where people always respect each other and work with a serious yet very joyful spirit. Whenever there is an urgent project, everyone always unites and supports each other regardless of position. This makes me feel like I am truly a part of the collective despite just being an intern.

**6. Policies / benefits for interns**  
The company provides internship allowance support and allows flexible time conditions when necessary. Additionally, being able to participate in internal training sessions is a big plus.

- I think the company could organize more periodic networking sessions or team building activities specifically for interns and project members.
